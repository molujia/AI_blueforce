# Urban Encirclement Baseline Rules

- Avoid known or suspected enemy fire zones when selecting a route.
- Prefer covered and concealed routes when approaching a target building.
- Before a building entry or encirclement step, run a situation assessment for entrances, enemy positions, and obstacles.
- Sudden contact is an immediate local emergency; the lower VBS/BT runtime should avoid, take cover, or continue according to local behavior without waiting for human review.
- If attack or fire authorization is requested, defer to the configurable HITL policy.
